Pedigree

What is pedigree

In the simplest sense, pet's pedigree is a number that represents the sum of rarities of pet's abilities. There are two types of pedigrees:
  • Manifested Pedigree - sum of rarities of manifested abilities
  • Total Pedigree - sum of rarities of all abilities in the pools

Pedigree is represented as: MANIFESTED PEDIGREE (TOTAL PEDIGREE) Example: 4 (20)

  • 4 - Manifested Pedigree - 2 Common Talents and 2 Common Derby Powers
  • 20 - Total Pedigree - 10 Common Talents and 10 Common Derby Powers

Ability Rarities

There are 5 Ability Rarities, each worth:
  • Common = +1 Pedigree
  • Uncommon = +2 Pedigree
  • Rare = +3 Pedigree
  • Ultra-Rare = +4 Pedigree
  • Epic = +5 Pedigree

Manifested Pedigree

Manifested Pedigree is a number that represents sum of rarities of all pet's MANIFESTED abilities. So sum of all rarities of Talents and Derby Powers your pet gains upon level up. Minimum (Baby pet): 0 * 0 + 0 * 0 = 0 + 0 = 0 (0 Common Talents and 0 Common Derby Powers) Minimum (Mega pet): 5 * 1 + 5 * 1 = 5 + 5 = 10 (5 Common Talents and 5 Common Derby Powers) Maximum: 5 * 5 + 5 * 5 = 25 + 25 = 50 (5 Epic Talents and 5 Epic Derby Powers)

Total/Potential Pedigree

Total Pedigree is a number that represents sum of rarities of ALL pet's abilities. So sum of all rarities of Talents that are in the pet's Talents pool and Derby Powers that are in the pet's Derby pool. Minimum: 10 * 1 + 10 * 1 = 10 + 10 = 20 (10 Common Talents and 10 Common Derby Powers) Maximum: 10 * 5 + 10 * 5 = 50 + 50 = 100 (10 Epic Talents and 10 Epic Derby Powers)
