Wizard's Companion

Jewels

What are pet jewels

Every pet unlocks an empty Star shaped jewel socket at Ancient level a.k.a. Pet Jewel Collar. In this socket you can socket a 6th pet ability of your choice.

Ability and jewel availability

Most talents and derby powers have an equivalent Jewel that grants that ability.
Some abilities can be only socketed in and not manifested directly.
There are even some premium Jewels that grant 2 talents at the same time. This type of jewels can only be acquired with real money.

Level requirements

Each Star jewel has a pet level requirement. Usually, the stronger the talent, the higher the pet's level needs to be in order for you to be able to socket it. For example:
  • Storm-Boon talent grants only +3% Storm Damage and its jewel requires level Ancient+.
  • Storm-Dealer talent grants +10% Storm Damage and its jewel requires level Ultra+.
This means that ideally you want to manifest talents whose jewels require Ultra+ so you won't need to take the pet to Ultra+ and then socket the 6th talent you want that requires only Ancient+, Epic+ or Mega+.

Jewel interactions with regular pet abilities

You can't socket a talent that the pet has already manifested.
If you socket a Talent at Ancient or Epic, this doesn't prevent the talent from manifesting on the pet when you level up the pet.
If the pet manifests the same talent that the socketed jewel grants, the jewel will X/cancel out and will no longer grant any benefit. Only the manifested talent will. After that, you will have to take the jewel out and replace it with a different one.

Replacing jewels

Most jewels are one time use only and shatter (get destroyed) if you try to take them out/replace them.
Premium jewels that you get as a reward by spending real money are usually Non-Shatter. That means when you try to take it out/replace it, it will unsocket instead of shattering and return to your backpack, ready to be used again, endlessly.

How to acquire

The most realiable ways to acquire Star jewels are:
  1. Crafting
  2. Creature drop
  3. Buying with real money

Crafting

Recipes are sold by Lowe Springfield located in Wizard City - Pet Pavilion - Pet Shoppe Boys Pet Shop. Each of these recipes produces 1 RANDOM jewel of said category (see the links for complete list of possible jewels).
The recipes include:
  • Derby Powers:
    • Rank 1 Derby Jewel - 100 Gold
    • Rank 2 Derby Jewel - 100 Gold
    • Rank 3 Derby Jewel - 100 Gold
    • Rank 4 Derby Jewel - 100 Gold
    • Rank 5 Derby Jewel - 100 Gold
  • Pet Statistic Boosting Talents:
    • Rank 2 Pet Stats Jewel - 500 Gold
    • Rank 3 Pet Stats Jewel - 500 Gold
    • Rank 4 Pet Stats Jewel - 500 Gold
  • Wizard Statistic Boosting Talents:
    • Pet Jewel of Balance - 1000 Gold
    • Pet Jewel of Death - 1000 Gold
    • Pet Jewel of Fire - 1000 Gold
    • Pet Jewel of Ice - 1000 Gold
    • Pet Jewel of Life - 1000 Gold
    • Pet Jewel of Myth - 1000 Gold
    • Pet Jewel of Storm - 1000 Gold
Each recipe requires:
  • X Pet Snacks you can buy from a Snack Vendor for Gold
  • X Metal-type reagents you can get as a drop from creatures or as a reward from feeding items to your pet
  • X Gemstone-type reagents you can buy from a Reagent Vendor for Gold

Creature drop

Most popular spot to farm pet jewels are Monkey Spiders in Empyrea - Aerial Jungle - Monkey Spider Cave. They drop a wide assortment of pet jewels, mostly school specific wizard statistic boosting talent jewels.
The drops are:
  • Wizard statistic boosting talent jewels for all schools
  • May-Cast Ally talent jewels
  • Univesal wizard statistic boosting talent jewels such as Spell-Proof, Sharp-Shot, Armor-Piercer, ...
For universal wizard statistic boosting talent jewels, your best chances are farming the Unforgiven Dead Gauntlet.
For Energy talent jewels, your best chances are farming the Forbidden Library Gauntlet.

Buying with real money

This includes:
  • Buying Scroll of Fortune and getting enough points to reach the goal that grants that jewel as a reward.
  • Buying Crowns/Membership gift cards.
Most popular choice of such jewels are jewels you get as a bonus from Kroger $20 USA Gift Card. What type of jewel you get is dictated by when you REDEEM (not buy) said gift card.
