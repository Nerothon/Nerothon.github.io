Wizard's Companion

PvP Battle Pass Rewards

Why bother with PvP Battle Pass for pet making

PvP Battle Pass offers some very EXCLUSIVE rewards for any serious Pet maker or anyone wanting to save time and money while making pets, that you can't reliably, easily and cheaply get anywhere else or can't get at all!

What are the rewards

The most desired items you can get out of PvP Battle Pass are:
  • Double Pet XP Elixir - 1 Hour
  • Purreau's Potion #9/Clear Hatching Slot Elixir
  • Energy Elixir
  • Elemental/Spiritual Retriever Tokens
  • Exclusive Pet - the pet changes every now and then. Past pets have included for example: Panther Kit-10, Jungle Kit-10, Cheetah Kit-10, ...

Where to find PvP Battle Pass

PvP Battle Pass can be found by interacting with any one of the pedestals in Wizard City - Unicorn Way - Arena - PvP Arena. Arena is located through the big door on the right side near the entrance next to Diego The Duelmaster. PvP Arena is located through the second tunnel from left between NPCs Jordo Mistborn and Marco Artigiano. There should be four pedestals right across the tunnel entrance spaced evenly. Interacting with any of them will show you menu similar to Events tab and Scroll of Fortune where you will find "PvP - Battle Pass" tab.

How to acquire the rewards

You have two choices:
  1. Participate in PvP matches to earn enough points to earn those rewards (Win = 3 points, Loss = 1 Point).
  2. Spend Crowns to Advance the PvP Battle Pass to desired Tier.

If you don't want to do PvP, only option is to buy these rewards with the second option. Advancing with is only worth for Tier 1 Elixirs and Tokens or Tier 3 for the Exclusive pet. If you want the Exclusive Pet, Advance to Tier 3 for 1260 but only once. You can make as many copies of the Exclsuive pet as you wish by hatching so it's not worth buying it multiple times. If you don't care about the Exclusive Pet and only care about the other rewards, Advance only to Tier 1 for 280. This will give you:

  • 1xPurreau's Potion #9 Elixir (normally worth alone 400 in the Crown Shop) - 7 point reward in Tier 1
  • 2xRetriever Tokens (that are otherwise very rare and hard to get) - 23 point reward in Tier 1
  • 1xDouble Pet XP Elixir - 1 Hour (which is impossible to buy anywhere else) - 29 point reward in Tier 1

You can do this on every character once per Battle Pass Season. If you need more Purreau's Potion #9 or Double Pet XP Elixirs and you have free character slot, you can create a new wizard, Advance their Battle Pass to Tier 1, claim the rewards, put them in your Shared Bank, delete the new wizard and repeat as many times as you need endlessly. There are more rewards in higher tiers and in the premium section of the Battle Pass (that you need to buy with to gain access to), but they are highly not worth it to Advance to if you don't play PvP and just want the rewards for cheap.
